About Ask Bob AI

Ask Bob AI is a free, AI-powered project planning assistant designed to help homeowners, DIY enthusiasts, and vehicle owners tackle projects with confidence. We bridge the gap between watching a quick tutorial and truly understanding how to plan and execute work safely and effectively.

Our Mission

To democratize practical knowledge by making expert-level project planning guidance accessible to everyone. We believe that understanding the "why" behind each step transforms people from uncertain beginners into confident, capable project planners who know when to DIY and when to call a professional.

Why Ask Bob AI Exists

The internet is full of DIY content, but most of it assumes you already know the basics or skips critical safety and code considerations. When you're planning a deck build, fixing brakes, or wiring a workshop, scattered YouTube videos and forum posts often leave you with more questions than answers.

Ask Bob AI was created to fill that gap. Instead of piecing together information from dozens of sources, you can have a conversation with Bob about your specific project. Bob asks clarifying questions about your space, experience level, and goals, then provides organized guidance that covers planning phases, material selection, tool requirements, safety considerations, and code compliance factors.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike generic search results or one-size-fits-all tutorials, Ask Bob AI provides contextual guidance tailored to your situation. Tell Bob about your 1970s ranch home, your intermediate carpentry skills, and your existing tool collection, and the guidance you receive will reflect those specifics.

We also emphasize safety and appropriate professional involvement. Bob doesn't encourage cutting corners on electrical work that requires permits or skipping jack stands to save time. When a project truly needs a licensed professional, Bob will tell you that directly rather than giving you overconfident advice that could lead to injury or code violations.

Our Values

Safety First

Every piece of guidance prioritizes user safety. We'd rather you hire a professional for complex work than attempt something dangerous based on our suggestions.

Honest Limitations

We're transparent about what AI guidance can and cannot do. Bob provides educational information, not professional engineering assessments or legally binding code interpretations.

Education Over Shortcuts

We explain the reasoning behind recommendations so you build lasting knowledge, not just complete a single project without understanding what you did or why.

Accessibility

Quality project planning guidance shouldn't require expensive consultations or knowing someone in the trades. Ask Bob AI is free to use for everyone.

The Team

Ask Bob AI was built by people who've spent time in garages, workshops, and job sites and who've experienced the frustration of trying to learn complex skills from scattered online resources. Our team combines hands-on trade experience with technology expertise to create an AI assistant that provides genuinely useful, practical guidance.

We've built decks, rewired shops, restored cars, and learned firsthand how valuable it is to have someone knowledgeable to ask questions. That experience shaped Bob's approach: practical guidance that respects your time and helps you make informed decisions rather than generic advice that leaves you guessing.

How We Sustain the Service

Ask Bob AI is free to use. We sustain the service through partnerships with tool and material suppliers, where relevant product recommendations may include affiliate links. These partnerships don't influence the educational guidance you receive. Bob's recommendations are based on what's appropriate for your project, not on commission rates.

We may also display advertisements from our advertising partners. These ads help support the ongoing development and maintenance of the service while keeping it free for all users. Ad content is clearly distinguished from our educational guidance.
