Many types of work legally require licensed professionals. Depending on your jurisdiction, this typically includes:

  • Electrical work beyond basic fixture replacement

Always verify licensing requirements with your local building department before beginning work. Performing work without required permits or licenses can result in fines, required removal of completed work, insurance complications, and safety hazards.

You are responsible for:
